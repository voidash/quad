
पहल सिंह लामा नेपाल प्रहरीको आठौ महानिरीक्षक हुन् । नेपाल प्रहरी क्लब in 1966 A.D. with the help of the then S.P. खड्गजीत बराल, Harka Bahadur Thapa and the then DIGP रोम बहादुर थापा, during his tenure as IGP. Pahal Singh Lama had been appointed Consul General of Calcutta, India by His Majesty's Government after his retirement from Nepal Police.
