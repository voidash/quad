Francis Xavier "The X-Man" Hernandez, is a former professional baseball player who pitched in the major leagues primarily in relief, from 1989-1998. He is currently the pitching coach for the Durham Bulls.
Xavier was named Texas High School Player of the Year in 1983 at Thomas Jefferson HS. He attended the University of Southwestern Louisiana.
Hernandez played for the Toronto Blue Jays, Houston Astros, New York Yankees, Cincinnati Reds and Texas Rangers .
